# as-is — how the work really happens

Mid-level index (progressive disclosure). Enter when you need to recover the real process — its exceptions and its workarounds — before anyone designs a replacement.

## Leaves
- `workaround-register.md` — mine the unhappy path; turn every "we just email Sven" into a requirement and a documented system failure. [CORE]
- `service-blueprint.md` — the as-is service blueprint weighted below the line of visibility, official SOP overlaid on the observed-real. [CORE]

## Fit
First and highest-value stop in the SKILL.md interview-assist loop, and the one the reversibility dial leans on most. The happy path is the version people will recite unprompted; the value and the risk both live on the unhappy path, backstage, in the steps the SOP omits. This branch reads the brief and any documented SOP going in, draws its probes from the concrete-instance rule, and hands the people branch its named human dependencies, the domain branch its shadow tools and contested terms, and the frame branch the recurring fail points that become the problem worth solving.
